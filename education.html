Impleamentation for improve education system

1. Community Involvement: Engage parents, teachers, local leaders, and community members to understand their needs and aspirations for education.

2. Infrastructure Development: Improve or build schools, libraries, and study spaces to provide conducive environments for learning.

3. Access to Resources: Ensure availability of textbooks, educational materials, and technology like computers or tablets.

4. Qualified Teachers: Recruit and train teachers who are passionate and qualified to teach, possibly through partnerships with nearby educational institutions.

5. Interactive Teaching Methods: Encourage interactive and participatory teaching methods to make learning engaging and effective.

6. Supplementary Programs: Offer extra-curricular activities, tutoring sessions, and mentorship programs to support students' holistic development.

7. Community Learning Centers: Establish community learning centers where people of all ages can access educational resources and engage in lifelong learning.

8. Parental Involvement: Encourage parents to be actively involved in their children's education through parent-teacher associations and workshops.

9. Utilize Local Resources: Integrate local culture, traditions, and resources into the curriculum to make learning relevant and meaningful for students.
